# Skill2Job

### Online Learning & Certification Platform

---

## Overview

**Skill2Job** is a full-stack e-learning platform designed to connect learners, trainers, and companies through training programs, certifications, and recruitment opportunities.

The platform allows users to access professional courses, participate in training sessions, pass exams, and connect with industry partners.

This project was developed as part of the **PIDEV – 4th Year Engineering Program at Esprit School of Engineering – Tunisia (Academic Year 2025–2026).**

---

# Features

## Sessions

- Create and manage training sessions
- Assign trainers to sessions
- Support for **online and onsite sessions**
- Participant registration and capacity management
- Weekly schedule for trainers

---

## HR

- User registration and authentication
- Role management (**Trainer / Student**)
- Trainer profile management

---

## Training Courses

- Create and manage courses
- Course enrollment
- Learning materials management
- Student progress tracking

---

## Exams & Certifications

- Certification exam management
- Question bank system
- Automatic grading
- Certification generation

---

## Partners

- Partner company management
- Industry collaborations
- Internship opportunities
- Job offers
